01 — Lead Conversion Flow

We capture every conversion — not just the ones who pay today.

The ambition is for people to transact online. For high-value home-energy purchases — solar, batteries, heat pumps — almost nobody buys a £8k install from a web form on day one. So rather than chase the rare full online sale, we are deliberately assumptive: every level of engagement is treated as a conversion.

We designed the enquiry flow around three distinct groups of people — bronze, silver and gold. By letting each one reach exactly as far as they're ready to go, we keep a follow-up open with all of them and maximise total conversion.

01
Bronze
Just curious about price

Price-curious browser

Very early in the journey — they just want a feel for cost. They reach a point in the flow where, by giving us their email address, they get an instant price for a recommended package.

Conversion captured
Email lead + indicative price → follow-up open
02
Silver
Seriously considering

Ready to plan, not pay

They go further — actually selecting a recommended package and booking a provisional installation date without paying a penny. That triggers a follow-up call to confirm the exact price.

Conversion captured
Package + provisional date → callback booked
03
Gold
Ready to commit

Ready to pay today

The minority who are ready to put money down. Finance applications and Stripe payment portals are available so they can act immediately, right inside the flow.

Conversion captured
Finance application / online payment
Why three streams

By viewing visitors as three distinct groups, we structure the flow to suit everybody individually. Nobody hits a dead end because they weren't ready to pay — we always come away with a contactable lead. That's how we maximise conversion instead of losing the 90% who'll never buy online on the first visit.
